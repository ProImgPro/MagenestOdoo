Authorization.

First of all, you need access right to use this attribute.

Go Settings ==> Users (Choose user who you want to have right)

Register FaceID.

You should register face your employee. From that , you will have features of faces.

Base on algorithm, your device can detect face correctly.

Go Employee App ==> Edit ==> Choose eye symbol and then click on it.

Other small screen will appear. Click on button "Take Snapshoot" and "Save & Close"

Base on algorithm, your device can detect face correctly.

Note: You should put you face near camera and camera should cover entire your face.
