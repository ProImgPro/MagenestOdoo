Features

  • Integrate AhaMove with Odoo.
  • As per requirement, Use AhaMove Connector in test and live environment.
  • Configure Domestic and International Delivery methods and services.
  • Domestic and International shipment will send in AhaMove
  • User can use the delivery method in sale order.
  • Send all shipping information from Odoo to AhaMove.
  • When Validate Delivery Order in Odoo, Label will Generated and Attached in Delivery Order.
  • User can able to generate multiple labels at a time in single delivery order.
  • Generate Live Shipment Tracking Number.
  • Reference Link AhaMove Api

Configuration.

First of all, you need to trigger this attribute in settings.

Go Sales ==> Settings (stick into AhaMove)

Add Shipping.

Add at least one product in order to use add shipping.

Remember , you must be setup your Dilevery and Warehouse address Correctly.

AhaMove will get those destinations to caculate price automatically.

Click to red area if you wanna change shipping method (see image above)

Next, you can change name of shipping method.

And must be fill in token.

Note: To get more information , you should contact with AhaMove provider.

Click to Get rate button and then AhaMove send you price of shipping immediately.

Now, you have link tracking your order use in the next section.

Delivery Products.

Click to smart button Delivery on top-right to delivery products.

Click on Validate button to confirm what you allow to delivery.

Click on Tracking smart button to track real time product shipping.

AhaMove provide you track real time. This attribute is so convenient for you.
